Founded in 1978, AKKA Group, is still a growing company operating the construction, tourism, food and retailing areas with a central office in Ankara, and two others in Istanbul and Antalya.

With high standard and modern architecture AKKA Construction’s projects have been points of references to the companies success story. There are different projects in AKKA constructions portfolio ranging from industrial complexes, public housing, tourism facilities and school constructions.

AKKA Group’s activities in the tourism sector has started with the construction of 3* Surtel Hotel, in the superior holiday district of Kuşadası. Later on hotels like the 5* Alinda Beach Hotel, 4* Park Claros Hotel, 5* Antendon De Luxe Hotel were put into action in the tourism paradise of Antalya. AKKA Hotels give its quests the opportunity of living unforgettable moments, in their unique facilities and quality policy that rise holiday pleasures to the maximum levels

AKKA Group, has entered the food sector with the Natural Spring Water Plantation in Çubuk, Ankara. The product is packed from its natural spring in a hygienic environment, in full automatic machines from its spring with a substantial production capacity, and brought to our tables under the Sağlık SU brand.

AKKA Group, has entered in retailing with Akkapark Alışveriş Mekezi in Kundu - Lara, Antalya answering the needs of both local and foreign tourists and presenting them a decent place were they can buy goods of good brands and shops. AKKA Group, will continue putting its name on the map with its future attacks for growing in the retailing sector.
